Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for spaghetti. It is a very delicious recipe. I will be making it every day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1 (6.5 ounce) can canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Place ground beef, onion, garlic, and crushed tomatoes in a large skillet. Cook over medium heat until meat is lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain fat and discard.
  2. Mix tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, oregano, salt, baking soda, and milk in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
